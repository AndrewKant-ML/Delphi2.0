• The system shall provide a personal role-based dashboard, after the login.
• When displaying the dashboard, the system shall provide the following functionalities:
  o For the students’ dashboard, taxes payments management, courses subscriptions management, exams reservations requests, and career management
  o For the professors’ dashboard, a list to the subscriptions to the led courses, exams dates insertion and students’ grades insertions (for each exam date)
• The system shall provide a list of taxes from the current academic year, either paid or not, with the possibility to pay then directly or to download the payment module
